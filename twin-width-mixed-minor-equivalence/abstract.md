This submission gives a full Lean proof that finite-graph twin-width and mixed
minor number are functionally equivalent: each parameter is bounded by a
numerical function of the other. Its concept surface has two review units. The
first gives the complete definition of mixed minor number, from interval
divisions and mixed matrix cells through vertex-ordered adjacency matrices.
The second states the functional equivalence with twin-width. The proof runs
through the Marcus–Tardos theorem, matrix Theorem 10, Theorem 14, and the graph
twin-decomposition bridge.

Ported from the original formalization by Édouard Bonnet
(github.com/EdouardBonnet/leaning, `twin-width`, MIT-licensed).
