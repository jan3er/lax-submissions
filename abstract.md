This submission formalizes two results of Marthe Bonamy and Colin Geniet:
every graph class of bounded merge-width is χ-bounded, and every such class
has linear neighbourhood complexity. The concepts define finite merge
sequences, radius-\(r\) merge-width, χ-boundedness, and neighbourhood
complexity; the proof package gives kernel-checked proofs of Theorems 1.2 and
1.5 of *χ-Boundedness and Neighbourhood Complexity of Bounded Merge-Width
Graphs* (arXiv:2504.08266).
